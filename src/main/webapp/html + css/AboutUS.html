About us

At Krosse Krabbe, we seek to offer a great experience with our burgers, where quality and flavor remain our absolute priorities. The quality of the products and our commitment to culinary innovation makes us stand out thanks to our exceptional burgers. Furthermore, we intend our restaurants to be not only a space to go to eat, we also desire them to be a place to create unforgettable moments.

Our History

Krosse Krabbe was born in the United States of America in the state of Massachusetts. From the beginning we had the desire to create a restaurant that stood out for its food but above all to create a welcoming space for families and friends. From day one, we set out to offer burgers of the excellentest quality, prepared with fresh and organic ingredients. Our innovative recipes guarantee that every bite is an explosion of flavor.

Our Philosophy

At Krosse Krabbe, we believe in the power of fresh and organic food. We are committed to using locally sourced ingredients and preparing each burger with care and dedication. We value sustainability and work with suppliers who share our vision of a healthier future. Another pillar of our restaurant is our compromise with our employees and ensures the best work environment.
